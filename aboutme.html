About D&D

D&D or Dungeons and Dragons is TTRPG (Tabletop role-playing game) where that players make characters and throw into a fantisy setting by the DM (Dungeons Master), and go a adventures and missions; it usaly ends when they have to fight the big bad evil guy at the end saving the world in some way.

The first addition of Dungeons and Dragons came out in 1974, and there where only three classes, claric fighting man and mage and no subclasses,now in one dnd the newst virsion there are 12 main classes and 48 subclasses; Thats a big jump!
